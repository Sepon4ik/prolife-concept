Proprietary Technologies

The science behind
every heartbeat.

Six technologies. Decades of clinical research. One device that sits on your bedside table.

01
AFibra® SMART

Detects atrial fibrillation during cuff inflation — clinically validated, first of its kind for home use.

02
ComfortPro

1.5× faster by measuring during inflation. Gentle and quiet — no long uncomfortable compression.

03
Intelligent Assistant

Risk indicators displayed at one press — structured, objective data every time you measure.

04
IHB Technology

Per-beat detection of irregular heartbeats that standard monitors simply miss.

05
AIm Technology

AI that analyses your first reading and personalises every subsequent measurement to you.

06
Mesh Technology

Micro-perforated cuff distributes pressure uniformly — no artifacts from movement.
